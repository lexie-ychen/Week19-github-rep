Redesign Mobile Desktop

Government Agency Redeisgn

The Canadian Food Inspection Redesign Project aims to enhance the user experience and interface of the Canadian Food Inspection Agency's online platform, focusing on improving aspects related to food safety and regulation. Initiated with a thorough assessment of usability through processes like heuristic evaluation and user testing, the project prioritizes consumer needs, leading to the development of wireframes and prototypes to optimize user experience in accessing crucial information on food safety and regulation.
